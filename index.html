Mira

AAC communication app for children

Getting Started

Mira helps children with communication needs express themselves by tapping symbol tiles that speak words and phrases.

Tap the gear icon on the home screen to enter Admin mode (default PIN: 1234). From there you can add categories, customize tiles, record custom voices, and adjust settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the PIN?
Go to Admin > Change PIN. Enter your current PIN, then set a new 4-digit PIN.

What is Mira Pro?
Mira Pro is a one-time $29.99 purchase that unlocks unlimited custom tiles, voice recording, usage analytics, and backup & restore.

Can I restore a purchase?
Yes — go to Admin > Mira Pro > Restore Purchase.

Are photos and recordings backed up?
No. For privacy, custom photos and voice recordings stay only on your device and are never included in backups.

What is the free tile limit?
The free version includes all 75 default tiles plus up to 20 custom tiles.
