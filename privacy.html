Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

Overview

Pearable ("we", "our", "the app") is an augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) app designed for children. We take the privacy of our users, especially children, very seriously. This policy explains what data Pearable collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Pearable does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

  • Photos and Audio: Custom tile photos and voice recordings are stored only in the app's private sandbox on your device. They are never uploaded to any server.
  • Usage Data: Tile tap events are logged locally for the optional usage analytics feature. This data never leaves your device.
  • Settings: Preferences such as grid size, appearance mode, speech rate, and PIN are stored locally using Apple's standard storage.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or account information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No analytics sent to third-party services
  • No cookies or web tracking

Camera and Photo Library

Pearable requests camera and photo library access only when you choose to add a custom photo to a tile. Photos are saved to the app's private storage, not to your device's camera roll. Access can be revoked at any time in your device's Settings.

Microphone

Pearable requests microphone access only when you record a custom voice for a tile (Pearable Pro feature). Audio recordings are saved locally within the app and are never transmitted externally.

In-App Purchase

Pearable Pro is a one-time purchase processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. Purchase management is handled through your Apple ID settings.

Backup and Export

The backup feature (Pearable Pro) creates a local archive file containing your categories and tile settings. This file stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it via AirDrop, Files, or another method. We never access your backups. Custom photos and voice recordings are intentionally excluded from backups for your privacy.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Pearable is designed for use by children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, no personal data leaves the device.

Third-Party Services

Pearable does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for purchase management.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored on your device. Deleting the app removes all associated data including custom photos, audio recordings, and settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
